Downloading audio from youtube.
Open youtube video page.
Right click on video.
Select and copy video URL.
Go to http://en.savefrom.net/
Enter youtube video URL to the input box.
Click blue right arrow button.
The box appears below.
In the box below click down arrow next to green download button.
Select "more".
Select "Audio MP4"
Right click on audio player.
Save audio as file on your computer.
You'll get m4a file.
Got to http://audio.online-convert.com/convert-to-ogg
Select local file under "Upload your audio you want to convert to OGG".
Click "Convert file" button below.
